- **Direct** - Straight to the point

## Content Principles Applied

1. **Lead with Data**: Always include specific numbers
2. **Be Direct**: No marketing fluff
3. **Show Contrast**: "18% vs 2%" not just "better"
4. **Promise Protection**: "Territory-protected" not just "quality"
5. **Respect Intelligence**: Assume users are smart

# Trust-Building Strategy for Skeptical Realtors

## The Problem
- Lead generation industry has a bad reputation
- Realtors have been burned by false promises
- Trust is at an all-time low
- Business model is considered "dead"

## The Solution: Radical Transparency + Risk Reversal

### 1. Money-Back Guarantee ⭐ Most Important
**Why it works**: Removes all risk for the realtor
- 30-day full refund, no questions
- Shows confidence in the product
- Proves we're not just taking money

### 2. No Long-Term Contracts
**Why it works**: Realtors hate being locked in
- Month-to-month only
- Cancel anytime
- We earn trust every month

### 3. Pay Per Lead (Preview Before Paying)
**Why it works**: Realtors see value before paying
- Preview leads in dashboard
- Only pay for what you accept
- No surprises

### 4. Full Transparency
**Why it works**: Realtors want to see the process
- See exact intent scores
- Read verification notes
- Understand the system
- No black boxes

### 5. Admit What We Don't Do
**Why it works**: Honesty builds trust
- "We don't promise 1000 leads"
- "We don't guarantee closings"
- "We don't resell leads"
- Shows we're different

### 6. Live Data, Not Testimonials
**Why it works**: Real numbers > fake reviews
- Show actual system metrics
- Real-time updates
- No cherry-picking
- Transparent reporting

### 7. Territory Protection
**Why it works**: Realtors fear competition
- One agent per territory
- No reselling
- Exclusive access
- Clear boundaries

### 8. Human Verification
**Why it works**: Realtors don't trust AI alone
- Every lead reviewed by humans
- Verification notes visible
- Quality over quantity
- Real people, real checks

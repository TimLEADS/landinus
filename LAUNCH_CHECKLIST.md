## Launch Tone

**We are opening carefully. Not scaling recklessly.**

- No aggressive marketing
- No volume promises
- No hype
- Focus on quality over quantity
- Respectful, intelligent communication
- System-driven, not sales-driven

## Success Metrics

### Trust Metrics
- Application completion rate
- User retention after first lead
- Feedback quality scores
- Territory balance maintained

### Quality Metrics
- Average intent score of delivered leads
- Conversion rate (agent-reported)
- Lead quality feedback scores
- System improvement suggestions

### System Health
- Error rate < 0.1%
- Page load times < 2s
- Uptime > 99.9%
- Support response time < 24h

---

**Remember:** The goal is not to scale fast. The goal is to build a system that agents trust, rely on, and build their business around.
